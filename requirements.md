**RESEARCH PROJECT – BASIC INFORMATION**

Prepare a presentation. 

Requirements:

- Follow the structure below.  
- Cite all your sources (include both in-text citations and a Works Cited page).  
- There should be at least 7 slides.  
- Your presentation should not be longer than 7 minutes.


Other important things to be aware of:

* clarity (speech & content)  
  * body language

Structure:

1) Research question (based on a broader topic)  
2) Background information  
- Explain the topic in simple, clear terms.  
- Give enough context so someone who isn’t familiar with the subject can understand your research question.  
- Show why the research is important or interesting.  
3) Methodology  
- Specify what types of materials you consulted (books, journal articles, reports, websites).  
4) Findings  
- Present the results of your study (what you found from analyzing the texts, such as patterns, themes, or key information).  
5) Conclusion  
- Interpret your findings (what it means) and answer your research question.  
6) a Works Cited page
