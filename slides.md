# Why Cats?

- **Companionship**: Independent yet affectionate.
- **Health Benefits**: Purring can lower stress and blood pressure.
- **Entertainment**: Endless funny antics.

---

# Cat Breeds

1. **Siamese**: Vocal and social.
2. **Maine Coon**: Gentle giants.
3. **Persian**: Fluffy and calm.
4. **Bengal**: Wild appearance, energetic.

# Communication

<!--
_class: lead
-->

> "Meow" is a language developed exclusively for humans.

- **Tail Position**: Vertical means happy!
- **Ears**: Flattened means scared or aggressive.
- **Purring**: Usually content, but sometimes self-soothing in pain.

---

# Fun Facts

- Cats sleep for ~70% of their lives.
- A group of cats is called a **clowder**.
- They can make over 100 different sounds (dogs make about 10).
